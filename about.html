About Project Gutenberg

Our Mission

"To encourage the creation and distribution of eBooks."

Project Gutenberg is a library of over 75,000 free eBooks. Choose among free epub and Kindle eBooks, download them or read them online. You will find the world's great literature here, with focus on older works for which U.S. copyright has expired.

History & Philosophy

Founded in 1971 by Michael S. Hart, Project Gutenberg is the oldest digital library. Hart’s mission was to provide as many eBooks in as many formats as possible for the entire world to read in as many languages as possible.
