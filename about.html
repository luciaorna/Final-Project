About Project Gutenberg

The First Provider of Free eBooks

"Project Gutenberg is a library of over 75,000 free eBooks. Choose among free epub and Kindle eBooks, download them or read them online."

Founded in 1971 by Michael Hart, Project Gutenberg is the oldest digital library in the world. Hart believed that the greatest value of computers should not be for computing, but for the storage, retrieval, and searching of what is stored in our libraries.

Free as in Freedom

The project focuses on older works for which U.S. copyright has expired. Thousands of volunteers have digitized and diligently proofread these works to ensure high quality for readers across the globe.
